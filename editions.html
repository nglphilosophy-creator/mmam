About the Series

The Moral Metaphysics at Maryland (MMaM) Workshop is a recurring workshop series held at the University of Maryland, College Park. It showcases new work on the nature of morality and other apparently normative domains—rationality, epistemology, social and political philosophy, and beyond—approached in the spirit of contemporary analytic metaphysics.

Series Organizers: Harjit Bhogal, Daniel Fogal, and N. G. Laskowski
